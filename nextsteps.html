What's Next?

Here's the next decade's work in a nutshell:

  • 2015 - Crowd-Sourcing Project (~$100k):

    • Establishment of a robust online community.
    • Creation of a non-profit entity for the project.
    • Consensus-building of pre-requisites for the village.

  • 2016 - Funding Round Two (~$2M):

    • Hiring of staff members for the project.
    • Start of professional design work.
    • Scouting for prospective town sites.

  • 2018-2020 - Funding Round Three (~$200M):

    • Purchase of ideal site and surrounding acreage.
    • Completion of architectural and design documents.
    • Selection of charter community members.
    • Start of physical construction of the community.
    • Establishment of the commonwealth's trust fund.

  • 2025 - Funding Round Four (~$1B):

    • Creation of five additional communities.
